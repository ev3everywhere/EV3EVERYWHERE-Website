Who We Are

EV3EVERYWHERE is a 501(c)3 nonprofit organization dedicated to promoting global access to STEM education and related technologies.

What We Do At Home

Here at EV3EVERYWHERE, we have developed a system where high school students can become robotics mentors and come to teachers’ aid, providing future leaders with an opportunity to give back to their community.

How We Help Globally

For those of our global community members who may not have access to STEM education and related technologies, we have created virtual learning labs providing students with internet access the ability to learn robotics.
