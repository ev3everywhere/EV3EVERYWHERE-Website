Frequently Asked Questions

What do mentors do?

Mentors work with elementary and middle school FIRST Lego League coaches and assist with the knowledge they have gained through prior experiences and our training seminars.

What requirements must aspiring mentors meet?

  • Mentors must be current high school students
  • Mentors must be able to leave school by 3:30 P.M
  • Mentors must have a reliable means of transportation
  • Mentors must be able to pass a background check

Why should you become a mentor?

Our mentors not only get an opportunity to give back to the community, but also get other perks

  • Community Hours
  • Leadership positions at local chapters
  • Job Opportunities as executives at EV3EVERYWHERE
  • Letters of Recommendation from partner elementary schools

Other Questions

For other questions, contact us at mentors@ev3everywhere.com
