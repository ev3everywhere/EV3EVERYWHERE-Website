We Make Change One Leap At A Time

About EV3EVERYWHERE

Mission Statement

To reinvent how young minds access and pursue Science, Technology, Art, Engineering, and Math.

What We Do

One of the greatest things about this modern age is the drive that young minds around the world have when they learn about engineering, science, and the arts. We saw a need to guide them so that they may one day be smart and intelligent leaders in the STEAM field. Our system involves creating a network of passionate high school students and dispatching them to be mentors for FIRST LEGO League teams in the community.

Several of our community members often don't have access to the expensive world of STEAM. The world moves forward because of those who work in the STEAM field. The world is fraught with antipathy, but we can make it better if educate and bring up everyone around us. Because of this, we are developing a robotics lab that allows students from around the world to learn about robotics and showcase their creations.
